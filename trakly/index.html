Trakly

The easiest and fastest way to
safely share your location.

Real-time location sharing with your friends

An Everyday App

Trakly can be used to coordinate a night out with friends, hanging out with your girlfriend or even find family members at a park.

Safe and Private

Control the amount of time you want to share your location with your friends. Your location automatically expires.

Invite by Text Message

Invite non-Trakly users via text message. They'll receive a link allowing them to view your ETA and location in real time.

Features

Simple and Fast.

Quickly create a Trakly to share your location with your friends and contacts. Pick a duration and a destination and hit done. That simple.

Your Friends in Real Time.

Forget the "where you at?" text message. Send a Trakly instead and directly see your friends on a map, their distances and ETAs.

Stay in Control.

In a second, change your travel mode, get the directions, add more friends and time. You can also leave the Trakly at anytime, to stop sharing your location.
